Lab-Dashboard

Interact with this is easy, just click in the colors and play around, his UI is intuitive

This dashboard is the end product of an automated data collection, transformation, and loading process. The business context is a drug research laboratory that outsources its analytical services. The graphs show the relationship between the inputs in the sample, the quantitative results, and the sales operations. The board tries to solve the resources of effort and time. Giving the result of:

  1. What was the most important physiochemical technology used for the laboratory?
  2. What was the most requested service and by whom?
  3. What is the most analyzed drug?
